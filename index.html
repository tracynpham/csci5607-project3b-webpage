required features

1. camera placement, changing in x, y, and z coordinates

2. film resolution (in 300x300, 300x500, and 1000x200)

3. aspect ratio, with images in 500x200 (fov angle of 35, 25, and 70)

4. user specified background colors (in orange, light blue, and pink)

5. lighting (ambient only, point light added in second photo)

6. spheres (multiple spheres in scene)

7. color and specularity (phong lighting model)

additional features we (attempted to) implemented

  • PNG output
  • Shadows
  • Multiple Light Sources
  • Refraction
  • Reflection
